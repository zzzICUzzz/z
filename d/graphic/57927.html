CT images of biologic lung volume reduction
Coronal computer tomography images obtained in the supine position at full lung inflation showing treatment site at baseline (pretreatment) and 6 weeks after low-dose (patients 1 to 4) and high-dose (patients 5 to 8) biologic lung volume reduction therapy. Numbering on the scans corresponds to treated sites showing evidence of scarring reactions.
BioLVR: biologic lung volume reduction; HD: high dose; LD: low dose; CT: computed tomography.
