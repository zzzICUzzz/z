Algorithm monitoring after surgical treatment of hydatidiform mole
During hCG monitoring, all patients should be using reliable contraception as a new pregnancy during this time would make it difficult or impossible to interpret hCG results and would complicate management.
hCG: human chorionic gonadotropin; GTN: gestational trophoblastic neoplasia.
