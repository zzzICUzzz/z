* Pharmacotherapy combined with nutritional rehabilitation and psychotherapy is indicated for bulimia nervosa. However, if nutritional rehabilitation and psychotherapy are not available, pharmacotherapy alone is reasonable. In addition, pharmacotherapy is indicated for comorbid anxiety disorders and depressive disorders.
