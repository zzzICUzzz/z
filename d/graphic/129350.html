Risk of sustained progression of retinopathy in patients receiving intensive therapy in DCCT
The glycated hemoglobin (A1C) values used were the mean of the values obtained every 6 months. The solid lines are regression lines estimate as a function of the log of the mean A1C value; the dashed lines are 95% confidence intervals.
