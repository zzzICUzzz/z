Reconstruction of digit tendon rupture
The patient suffereda flexor tendon rupture in the left little finger (A).Due toretraction ofthetendon at the rupture site and poor condition of the tendonsheath, a silicon tendon rodwas initially placed (B). After two months, the tendon was reconstructed using a palmaris longus tendon graft (C). Following recovery, the patient demonstrated good range of motion with extension (D) and flexion of the little finger(E).
