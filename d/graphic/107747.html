Types of bronchoscopes
Bronchoscopes in increasing size are displayed. An ultrathin bronchoscope is shown on the left while an endobronchial ultrasound (EBUS) scope is shown on the right. The remaining pieces of equipment are routine bronchoscopes of two different sizes. The ultrathin and routine bronchoscopes have a light source, a camera, and a suction port seen at the tip.
