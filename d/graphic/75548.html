Ovarian hyperthecosis
Micrograph of hematoxylin- and eosin-stained ovarian tissue (200), demonstrating nests of luteinized theca cells (surrounded by dashed line) within the ovarian stroma. The cells are larger and lighter in appearance than the surrounding stromal cells, which are spindle shaped.
