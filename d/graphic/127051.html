Materials to perform a bougie-assisted cricothyrotomy (scalpel-finger-bougie technique)
The bougieassisted cricothyrotomy, also known as the scalpel-finger-bougie technique, is a relatively simple technique requiring only a few tools, including a scalpel, tracheal tube introducer ("bougie"), syringe, and tracheal tube.
