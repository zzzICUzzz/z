Morphea profunda

Morphea profunda
(A) Circumscribed erythematous plaque on the left side of the back.
(B) Histopathology showing fibrosis of the mid-dermis to subcutaneous tissues with mononuclear cell infiltration with focal lymphoid follicles in the lower dermis (hematoxylin-eosin stain, original magnification 20).
(C) Immunohistologic examination shows lymphoid aggregates positively stained for CD20.
From: Ito T, Mori T, Miura T, Yamamoto T. Pediatric-onset solitary morphea profunda. Int J Dermatol 2021; 60:e116. https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/ijd.15078.
