Clinical management of unsatisfactory cervical cytology

This algorithm describes the steps involved in clinical management of unsatisfactory cytology. Note that "unknown genotype" refers to both HPV testing without genotyping and HPV testing where genotyping is negative for HPV 16 and 18 but positive for other high-risk HPV types.
Patients <25 years inadvertently screened for HPV may be managed differently; see related UpToDate content.

HPV: human papillomavirus; ASCCP: American Society for Colposcopy and Cervical Pathology.
