Axial spin echo MRI in a normal subject in the retropalatal region. The tongue, soft palate, parapharyngeal fat pads, lateral parapharyngeal walls, parotid gland, subcutaneous fat, teeth, and mandibular rami are all highlighted on this image.
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
