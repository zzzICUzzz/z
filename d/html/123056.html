 TI-RADS interpretation

 
Points Category Designation Recommendation
0 to 1 TR1 Benign No FNA
2 TR2 Not suspicious No FNA
3 TR3 Mildly suspicious FNA if lesion ≥2.5 cm or follow if lesion ≥1.5 cm
4 to 6 TR4 Moderately suspicious FNA if lesion ≥1.5 cm or follow if lesion ≥1 cm
7 to 17 TR5 Highly suspicious FNA if lesion ≥1 cm or follow if lesion ≥0.5 cm

 
Notes
  • Ultrasonographic classification applies directly to the population investigated and only indirectly, and imperfectly, to a specific patient.
  • FNA: fine-needle aspiration.
