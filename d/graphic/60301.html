Fibroblast on electron microscopy
Fibroblast viewed by electron microscopy. This elongated cell, with multiple, delicate cell processes and an oval nucleus, is an active fibroblast. During the repair reaction, fibroblasts secrete extracellular matrix components. This activity is manifested by the distended cisternae of the rough endoplasmic reticulum (arrows).
