Leiomyosarcoma: Gross appearance compared with leiomyoma
Leiomyoma and leiomyosarcoma. The small nodule on the left is well circumscribed, with the bulging, white, firm, and whorled cut surface, typical of leiomyoma. The large, soft, hemorrhagic, and fleshy mass represents a leiomyosarcoma.
