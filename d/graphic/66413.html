Development of the digestive organs
Origin of the accessory organs of digestion.
(A-B) The accessory organs of digestion (liver, gallbladder, and pancreas) first emerge as buds of the foregut tube in the space provided by the ventral mesentery.
(C-E) As the organs enlarge and move, the foregut mesentery goes with them and persists in the same way that the dorsal mesentery does.
