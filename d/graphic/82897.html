Yergasons test for biceps tendon
Yergasons test is used to assess the biceps tendon. ItÂ is performed with the patient's arm pronated and flexed at the elbow to 90 degrees. The patient then attempts to supinate the arm against a resisted isometric force applied by the examiner. Pain localized to the long biceps tendon marks a positive test.
