Hairy cell leukemia in peripheral blood
Peripheral smear from a patient with hairy cell leukemia. Wright-Giemsa stain showing two hairy cells. The cells have abundant, irregularly distributed cytoplasm with irregular cytoplasmic outlines, giving the cells their "hairy" appearance. The nuclei vary from round to oval.
