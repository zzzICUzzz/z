Necrolytic migratory erythema (Glucagonoma syndrome)
Inflammatory dermatosis with angular cheilitis, and inflammatory, scaly, and erosive plaques around the nose, mouth, and medial aspects of the eyes (left panel). The right panel depicts polycyclic erosions in the anogenital region with necrotic flaccid epidermis still covering part of these erosions.
