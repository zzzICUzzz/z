Echo 3D right ventricle
Starting from a full-volume acquisition, the RV endocardial border is semi-automatically contoured in orthogonal planes (panels A, B, and C) and, using a surface modelling algorithm, a 3-dimensional volumetric representation of the RV is generated (panel D). Global and regional RV-end diastolic and end-systolic volumes are reported, along with RV ejection fraction.
RV: right ventricular.
