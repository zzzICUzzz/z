High power viewof biopsies of the colon in a patient with cytomegalovirus (CMV)colitis revealssevere acute and chronic inflammation.A lesional cell (circle) with characteristic cytomegaly and both nuclear and cytoplasmic inclusions is diagnostic of a CMV infection. CMV inclusions are usually found in stromal and endothelial cells and rarely in epithelial cells.
