Reconstruction after burn scar excision
Sequence of reconstruction of a lower extremity defect following symptomatic burn scar excision.An osmotic tissue expander was inserted adjacent to theburn scar (A). Once sufficient expanded tissue was obtained, the osmotic tissue expander was removed (B). The burn scar was excised (C). The defect after scar excision (D) was reconstructed using anadvancement flap of the expanded skin (E). The final appearance ofthe wound is shown in(F).
