Correlation between the five-layer echo pattern and histologic layers of the normal gastrointestinal wall
On endoscopic ultrasound, the normal intestinal wall is visualized as five alternating hyperechoic (bright) and hypoechoic (dark) bands that correspond to five distinct histologic layers of the intestinal wall.
