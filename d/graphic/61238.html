Tumbling motility of Listeria monocytogenes
Hanging drop slide of an overnight brain heart infusion broth inoculated with Listeria monocytogenes. In the video, the bacteria rotate slowly in and out of focus around a central axis; a characteristic and specific movement pattern referred to as "tumbling motility" (magnification 400x).
