Pleural pressure volume curve in trapped lung and heart failure
  • Pressure/volume curves are plotted in which the cause of the pleural effusion was attributed to a trapped lung resulting from different pleural insults with concomitant heart failure.
  • The solid circles and solid triangles represent two separate patients with heart failure and trapped lung from uremic pleurisy; the solid squares represent a patient with heart failure and trapped lung following coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG).
