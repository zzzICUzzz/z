Notes
  • The BASDAI is a measure of disease activity in patients with spondyloarthritis. Values of at least 4 (on a scale from 0 to 10) are consistent with high disease activity. A 2-point reduction in the score resulting from therapy is considered a significant response to treatment.
  • AS : ankylosing spondylitis.


Equations used

BASDAI = ((Q1 + Q2 + Q3 + Q4) + ((Q5 + Q6) / 2)) / 5
