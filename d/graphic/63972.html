Difference between matrix and heterotopic calcification
A) Enchondroma of the distal femur demonstrates flocculent matrix resembling rings, or Cs and Os. Calcification is concentrated in the center of the lesion.
B) Heterotopic calcification in a bone infarct. The calcification is mainly in the periphery of the lesion and has a wavy or serpentine appearance.
