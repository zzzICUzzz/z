Circumferential urethral prolapse

(A) This young girl developed urethral prolapse, manifesting with the characteristic doughnut sign, demonstrated here as a swollen round tissue with a central dimple that is smooth and friable.

(B) The smooth doughnut shape and central lumen are characteristic features of a urethral prolapse, which, if large or swollen, often conceals the vagina below it.
