Parasagittal oblique interlaminar view for lumbar neuraxial ultrasound
In the parasagittal oblique view, the interlaminar space is visualized and contains the posterior complex (ligamentum flavum/posterior epidural space/dura) and anterior complex (posterior longitudinal ligament/vertebral body).
