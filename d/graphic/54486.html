The three signals of T cell activation
Signal I: Antigen presentation by APC to the T cell receptor.
Signal II: Costimulation - binding of additional APC ligands to specific T cell receptors.
Signal III: Newly synthesized IL-2 and growth factors feed back on T cell membrane receptors, causing clonal expansion of newly activated T cells.
