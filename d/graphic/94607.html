Characteristic facial features in a child with fetal alcohol spectrum disorder
Examples of the fetal alcohol syndrome facial phenotype across three races: White American, Native American, and African American. Characteristic facial features include short palpebral fissure length, smooth philtrum, and a thin upper lip.
