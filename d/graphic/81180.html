Live birth following clomiphene citrate
A two-step nomogram predicting chances for live birth following clomiphene citrate on the basis of initial screening characteristics.
BMI: body mass index; FAI: free androgen index; T: testosterone; SHBG: sex hormone-binding globulin.
Reproduced with permission from: Imani, B, Eijkemans, MJ, te Velde, ER, et al. A nomogram to predict the probability of live birth after clomiphene citrate induction of ovulation in normogonadotropic oligoamenorrheic infertility. Fertil Steril 2002; 77:91.
