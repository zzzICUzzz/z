Survival according to T category and the number of positive nodes for patients undergoing resection of exocrine pancreatic cancer, stratified according to the revised AJCC 8th edition TNM staging criteria
(A) Overall survival by T stage of 525 patients who underwent resection for node-negative pancreatic cancer, stratified by proposed AJCC 8th edition criteria (training set only).
(B) Overall survival by number of positive nodes for all patients who underwent a R0 resection (training set, n = 1551), stratified by proposed AJCC 8th edition criteria.
T: tumor; AJCC: American Joint Committee on Cancer; TNM: tumor, node, metastasis; R0: no residual tumor.
