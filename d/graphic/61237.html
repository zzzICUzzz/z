Acute inferior and right ventricular myocardial infarction
Electrocardiogram shows Q waves and prominent doming ST segment elevation in II, III, and aVF, findings which are characteristic of an acute inferior myocardial infarction. ST elevation in the right precordial leads V4R, V5R, and V6R indicates right ventricular involvement as well (arrows). The ST depressions in leads I and aVL represent reciprocal changes.
