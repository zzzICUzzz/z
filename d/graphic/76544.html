MRI findings in diabetic lumbar plexopathy
MR neurography coronal images show increased caliber and T2 signal (A, B) and abnormal T1 contrast enhancement (C, D) of the right L3 nerve (short thin arrow), L4 nerve (long thin arrow), proximal portion of the femoral nerve (short thick arrow), and right obturator nerve (arrowheads).
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
