Nutrition support protocol in burn patients
TBSA: total body surface area; TENS: toxic epidermal necrolysis syndrome; SJS: Stevens-Johnson syndrome; FT: feeding tube (nasoenteral, nasogastric).
* Indirect calorimetry performed every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.
