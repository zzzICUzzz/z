Flow cytometric histograms in B cell acute lymphoblastic leukemia
Composite flow cytometry histograms showing immunophenotypic profile of a precursor B cell acute lymphoblastic leukemia (ALL). Note that the cells are dim CD45+ (A), TdT+ (B), CD34- (C), and coexpressing CD10 and CD19 (D).
