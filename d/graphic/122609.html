Doppler waveforms in types 1, 2, and 3 sFGR
(A) Type 1 sFGR. Persistently forward umbilical artery end-diastolicflow without variation in the waveformand normal or elevated resistance.
(B) Type 2 sFGR. Fixed absent or fixed reversed umbilical artery end-diastolicflow without variation of the waveform in the smaller twin.
(C) Type 3 sFGR. Variable umbilical artery flow patternthat cycles between forward, absent, and reversed flow over a short interval.
sFGR: selective fetal growth restriction.
