Pad placement for defibrillation
Vector change defibrillation consists of altering the direction of the vector. In clinical practice, this typically means moving the electrode pads from their standard position to an anterior-posterior position, as shown above. Double sequential external defibrillation (DSED) consists of rapid sequential shocks from 2 defibrillators.
DSED: double sequential external defibrillation; VC: vector change.
