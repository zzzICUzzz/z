Pneumothorax size estimated by the average intrapleural distance
The schematic diagram details the use of the average intrapleural distance (in centimeters) to estimate the size of a pneumothorax from a frontal chest radiograph. The average of A + B + C (in centimeters) is applied to the scale to determine pneumothorax size as a percentage (% Ptx).
