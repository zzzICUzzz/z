Diffusion-weighted imaging lesion patterns of 11 patients with migrainous infarction. Patients 1 through 4 have multiple small lesions, patients 5 through 8 have isolated lesions in the posterior circulation territory, and patients 9 through 11 have isolated lesions in the middle cerebral artery territory.
