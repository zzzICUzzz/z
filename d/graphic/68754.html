Photonumeric scale for cutaneous photodamage
Photonumeric scale used for the assessment of cutaneous photodamage. Five photographic standards (en face and 45 degree oblique) illustrating increasing severity of photodamage, where grade 0 represents no damage; grade 2, mild damage; grade 4, moderate damage; grade 6, moderate/severe damage; and grade 8, severe damage.
