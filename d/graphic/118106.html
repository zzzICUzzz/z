Mean change in blood pressure by quintile of weight change at 36 months in phase 2 of the Trials of Hypertension Prevention (TOHP)
White bars represent diastolic blood pressure and gray bars represent systolic blood pressure. Error bars represent 95% CI. Data are adjusted for age, ethnicity, and sex according to quintile of weight change at 36 months (weight loss and usual care groups combined).
From: Annals of Internal Medicine, Stevens VJ, Obarzanek E, Cook NR, et al. Long-term weight loss and changes in blood pressure: results of the Trials of Hypertension Prevention, phase II.
