Nail bracing for ingrown toenail
Representative cases showing effectiveness of nail brace application in children with ingrown nails. Erythema and swelling were significant before treatment and were alleviated after nail brace application in 4 to 10 weeks (indicated by arrows, in time sequence from left to right).
From: Shih YH, Huang CY, Lee CC, Lee WR. Nail Brace Application: A Noninvasive Treatment for Ingrown Nails in Pediatric Patients. Dermatol Surg 2019; 45:323. DOI: 10.1097/DSS.0000000000001530.
