Heel contusion taping
Patients recovering from a heel contusion may benefit from additional support, such as that provided by the taping described here:
(A) Apply anchors behind and below heel.
(B) Using a basket weave technique, apply strips until heel is covered.
(C) Reapply anchors to finish.
