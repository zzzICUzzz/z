Our approach to initial triage and home management of asthma exacerbations in adults and adolescents
This algorithm is designed for a medical professional providing advice to a patient who is at home and has an asthma exacerbation. More than one phone call may be needed to complete the algorithm.

PEF: peak expiratory flow; MDI: pressurized metered dose inhaler; SpO2: pulse oxygen saturation; DPI: dry powder inhaler; FABA: fast-acting beta agonist (eg, albuterol, formoterol-budesonide).

* Comorbid conditions that may complicate asthma exacerbation:
  • Acute bronchitis
  • Acute bacterial sinusitis
  • Anaphylaxis
  • Heart failure, arrhythmias
  • Influenza, COVID-19
  • Pneumonia
  • Pneumothorax
