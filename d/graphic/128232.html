Ultrasound of preterm infant with grade 3 cerebellar hemorrhage and bilateral GMH-IVH
Ultrasound in a preterm infant (gestational age 24 weeks) with unilateral grade 3 CBH (arrows) and bilateral grade 3 GM-IVH (arrowheads). Coronal anterior (A), right parasagittal anterior (B), left coronal mastoid (C), and right coronal mastoid (D) fontanel views demonstrate CBH as an area of increased echogenicity involving more thanone-third of the right cerebellar hemisphere. The lesion is shown more clearly on the mastoid fontanel views.
CBH: cerebellar hemorrhage; GMH-IVH: germinal matrix hemorrhage and intraventricular hemorrhage.
