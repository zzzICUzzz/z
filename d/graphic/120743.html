Strategies for optimizing cardiac resynchronization therapy
Optimization of CRT can be achieved by avoiding scar (B), targeting a Q-LV interval 95 ms (C), employing algorithms to maximize fusion of LV pacing with native conduction (D), and pacing at multiple LV sites (E).
LV: left ventricular; CRT: cardiac resynchronization therapy.
