Histologic changes in the esophageal muscular layer of MCTD patients
Histological changes in the esophageal muscle layer of MCTD patients. Muscular changes in the esophagus were particularly marked in the inner circular muscular layer.
(A) Slight degeneration of muscular tissue at a very early stage.
(B) Atrophy and degeneration at a relatively early stage.
(C) Degeneration of muscular tissue with brosis.
(D) Severe atrophy and degeneration of muscular tissue with brosis involving almost the entire muscular layer.
Original magnication: upper, 20; lower, 200. Elastica-Masson stain.
MCTD: mixed connective tissue disease.
Reproduced from: Uzuki M, Kamataki A, Watanabe M, et al. Histological analysis of esophageal muscular layers from 27 autopsy cases with mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD). Pathol Res Pract 2011; 207:383. Illustration used with the permission of Elsevier Inc. All rights reserved.
