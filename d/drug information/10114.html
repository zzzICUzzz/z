Use: Labeled Indications

Dietary supplement: Essential amino acid that regulates immune response helping to manage stress in body.

Pregnancy Considerations

L-lysine crosses the placenta in humans (Ronzoni, 1999; Schneider, 1979). Lysine is an essential amino acid. The RDA for lysine is increased in pregnant women (IOM, 2005).

Breastfeeding Considerations

Lysine is an essential amino acid and is found in breast milk. The RDA for lysine is increased in breast-feeding women (IOM, 2005).
