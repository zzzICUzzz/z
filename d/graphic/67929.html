The color Doppler echocardiogramÂ of a patient with mild tricuspid regrugitation (TR) due to moderate pulmonary hypertension shows a TR jet; although the jet entrains along the interatrial septum (IAS), it has a narrow vena contracta and proximal acceleration region (PISA), suggesting that TR is mild (panel A).
