Noninvoluting congenital hemangioma (NICH)
An 8 cm diameter soft compressible blue subcutaneous mass with overlying coarse telangiectasias and surrounding pallor on the arm of a 10-year-old girl. This soft-tissue mass was unchanged since birth.
