Notes
  • *ECOG performance status (PS) 0 or 1: Fully active or only restricted in strenuous physical activity; no performance restrictions, or fully ambulatory and able to carry out light work. [1]
  • The CNS-IPI was developed using data from >2000 patients (age 18 to 80 years) with aggressive B-cell lymphomas (80 percent DLBCL) treated with rituximab and CHOP-like chemotherapy, who were enrolled in prospective studies and analyzed for occurrence of relapse/progression in the CNS; the resulting risk model was validated with an independent data set of >1500 patients with DLBCL. [2]
  • For information on the use of the CNS-IPI in DLBCL, refer to the UpToDate topic review on Initial treatment of limited-stage DLBCL.
  • CNS : central nervous system; DLBCL : diffuse large B-cell lymphoma; R-CHOP : rituximab, cyclophosphamide, doxorubicin, vincristine, prednisone; ECOG : Eastern Cooperative Oncology Group performance; LDH : lactate dehydrogenase.


References
  1. Oken MM, Creech RH, Tormey DC, et al. Toxicity and response criteria of the Eastern Cooperative Oncology Group. Am J Clin Oncol 1982; 5:649.
  2. Schmitz N, Zeynalova S, Nickelsen M, et al. CNS International Prognostic Index: A risk model for CNS relapse in patients with diffuse large B-cell lymphoma treated with R-CHOP. J Clin Oncol 2016; 34:3150.
