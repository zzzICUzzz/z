Bronchoscope with rotation ring
Flexible bronchoscope with the ability to rotate the insertion tube at the handle. The rotation ring allows additional 120 degrees of axial movement to allow increase maneuverability to access subsegmental bronchi and/or upper lobe segments. The outer diameter is 5.5 mm with a working channel of 2 mm.
