Ischemic monomelic neuropathy
Typical appearance of ischemic monomelic neuropathy with wrist drop and claw hand. The patient had a failed radiocephalic AV fistula and developed symptoms after placement of a brachial-based AV graft.
AV: arteriovenous.
From: Scher LA, Shah AR. Vascular access neuropathic syndrome: Ischemic monomelic neuropathy. In: Vascular Access: Principles and Practice, 5th ed, Wilson SE (Ed), Wolters Kluwer, Philadelphia 2009.
