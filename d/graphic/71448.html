Extension injury to olecranon with valgus stress
(A) A fall on an outstretched hand with the elbow extended places a valgus stress on the forearm.
(B) This stress can cause a greenstick fracture of the olecranon with or without a radial neck fracture or medial epicondylar apophysis avulsion.
(C) Radiograph of a valgus extension fracture of the olecranon with an associated radial neck fracture.
