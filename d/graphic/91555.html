ILO description of radiographic features of pneumoconioses A
Synopsis of parameters and additional symbols that are noted and graded on the International Labour Organization (ILO) pneumoconiosis form.
R: regular; I: irregular; p, q, r, s, t, u: these designations refer to the size and shape of nodules; qq: all nodules are described by q (small, rounded nodules with diameter 1.5 up to about 3 mm); tt: all nodules are described by t (irregular nodules with diameter 1.5 up to about 3 mm); qt: mix of q and t with q predominating; tq: mix of t and q nodules with t predominating; RUZ: right upper lung zone size.
