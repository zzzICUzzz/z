Iliotibial band (ITB) function
The illustrations above demonstrate how the function of the iliotibial band (ITB) depends on the position of the knee:
(A) With the knee in full extension, the ITBacts as an active knee extensor.
(B) At 20 to 30 of knee flexion, the ITBbecomes an active knee flexor.
