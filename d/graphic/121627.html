Subclassification of patients with class 1 ADPKD
Subclassification of patients with class 1 ADPKD at baseline by Irazabal et al based on htTKV limits for their age. Limits are defined based on estimated kidney growth rates of 1.5, 3.0, 4.5, and 6.0%.
ADPKD: autosomal dominant polycystic kidney disease; htTKV: height-adjusted total kidney volume (mL/m).
Reproduced with permission from: Irazabal MV, Rangel LJ, Bergstralh EJ, et al. Imaging classification of autosomal dominant polycystic kidney disease: A simple model for selecting patients for clinical trials. J Am Soc Nephrol 2015; 26:160.
