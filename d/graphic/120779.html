Prevalence of high levels of microsatellite instability (MSI-H) in tumor/normal paired samples across 39 human cancer types
(A) Microsatellite instability prevalence was detected across 39 tumor types.
(B) The relative level of instability, as measured by MANTIS score, is shown across all 39 tumor types. Note that for CLL, the listed microsatellite instability prevalence in panel A is out of 279 patients, and all 338 tumors are shown in panel B. The MANTIS threshold cutoff of 0.4 is depicted with a dashed line.
