Anterior knee dislocation with PCL intact
The lateral knee radiograph above shows an anterior knee dislocation with the posterior cruciate ligament (PCL) intact. Note the parallel alignment of the patellofemoral joint with the femur in the complete bicruciate injury and the close proximity of the femur and tibia.
