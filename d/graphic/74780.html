Modified seldinger technique for central venous access
(A) The needle is inserted into the selected vein. (B) The flexible end of the guidewire is passed through the needle and advanced into the vessel. (C) The needle is removed, leaving the guidewire in place. (D) The catheter is advanced over the guidewire with a twisting motion into the vessel. A vascular dilator should be passed over the wire prior to placement of a large catheter. (E) The guidewire is removed while the catheter is held in place.
