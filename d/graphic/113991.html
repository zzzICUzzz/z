Minimum alveolar concentration (MAC) chart for isoflurane
MAC is the minimum alveolar concentration of an inhalation anesthetic agent that prevents 50% of patients from moving in response to a surgical stimulus.
MAC: minimum alveolar concentration; N2O: nitrous oxide.
