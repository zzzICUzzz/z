Simple versus complex dorsal metacarpophalangeal dislocations of the thumb
Simple and complex dorsal dislocations of the thumb MCP joint. Simple dislocations (A) are in extension and reducible. Complex dislocations (B) are in bayonet apposition and are irreducible because of the interposed volar plate.
