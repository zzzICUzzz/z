Contrast enema image of transverse colon cut-off due to midgut volulus
Radiograph obtained with a barium enema shows a beak-like disruption of the midtransverse colon (arrow). At surgery for reduction of the volvulus, the colon was located mostly on the left side, consistent with malrotation.
