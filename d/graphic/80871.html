Aneurysmal bone cyst of the proximal fibula
Frontal (A) and lateral (B) views demonstrate an expanded lesion of the diametaphyseal area of the fibula. The lesion is trabeculated and does not cross the physis. This lesion is radiologically characteristic enough to allow a confident diagnosis.
