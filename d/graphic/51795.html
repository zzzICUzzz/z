Chest computed tomograph of a patient with coccidioidomycosis
High-resolution computed tomography (CT) image (1.5-mm collimation) shows thin-walled cavity in right upper lobe. The diagnosis of coccidioidomycosis was made at surgical resection. The patient was a 44-year-old man who developed coccidioidomycosis following travel to an endemic area.
