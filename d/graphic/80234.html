Larynx appearance with videolaryngoscope
Several photographs of the larynx obtained with a videolaryngoscope: (A) preintubation view of an adult airway; (B) preintubation view of a pediatric airway; (C) endotracheal tube passing between the vocal cords; (D) view of the larynx and hypopharynx (note the horizontally oriented space below the laryngeal inlet, which is the esophagus).
