Effects of testosterone or placebo treatment for 12 months on volumetric bone mineral density
Bars indicate means, and error bars indicate standard deviations. The p values indicate the significance of the difference in change in percent vBMD from baseline to 12 months for men in the testosterone arm compared with the placebo arm.
vBMD: volumetric bone mineral density.
Reproduced with permission from: Snyder PJ, Kopperdahl DL, Stephens-Shields, AJ, et al. Effect of testosterone treatment on volumetric bone density and strength in older men with low testosterone: A controlled clinical trial. JAMA Intern Med 2017; 177(4):471-79.
