Allergic contact dermatitis induced by neomycin ointment
A red, rectangular, scaly, and excoriated 2 cm plaque with surrounding scattered red papules and intac wound with sutures in place. An 8-year-old girl developed an acute allergic contact dermatitis following the application of neomycin to prevent infection after excision of a congenital pigmented nevus.
