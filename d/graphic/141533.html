Bilateral schizencephaly
Bilateral schizencephaly (type 3). MR images of a 2-year old child with global developmental delay. Axial T2-weighted (A, B) and coronal reconstructions from T1-weighted volume data (C-F) show bilateral CSF clefts with nonopposed borders lined with polymicrogyria. The septum pellucidum is absent and the fornices lie abnormally low (arrows in C-F). Extensive cortical formation abnormalities were present in both hemispheres.
MR: magnetic resonance; CSF: cerebrospinal fluid.
