Gastrocnemius tear hematoma ultrasound
The top image is a longitudinal ultrasound scan of themedial calf showing a14-cm-long hematoma (asterisk)associated with ahigh-grade medial gastrocnemius (delta)tear. A distal tear (arrow) is seen at the myofascial junction. The bottom image is a transverse view of the same tear and hematoma (asterisk) and shows that it extends across the entire gastrocnemius (delta).
Originally published in: Fields KB, Rigby MD. Muscular calf injuries in runners. Curr Sports Med Rep 2016; 15:320. Reproduced with permission. Image may only be used with permission from Karl B. Fields, MD.
