Computed tomography of allergic fungal rhinosinusitis with bone erosion
Coronal computed tomography (soft tissue windows) demonstrates AFRS of both sphenoid sinuses with multiple areas of bone erosion (arrows). Note the differential densities consistent with fungal sinusitis.
