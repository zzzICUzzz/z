Lateral non-weight-bearing radiograph of right foot
Lateral non-weight-bearing radiograph of a right foot, showing a normal dorsal cortical line (white line) from the first metatarsal to the medial cuneiform. Any step-off along this tangential may signify instability of the first tarsal-metatarsal joint.
