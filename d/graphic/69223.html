Continuous combinations
May use any monophasic 21/7 combination (eg, Amethyst [levonorgestrel 0.09 mcg-ethinyl estradiol 20 mcg]) by taking active hormone pills for 28 or more days continuously. Any progestin may be used, and higher doses of ethinyl estradiol may be used in some women. Refer to UpToDate topic.

  • Annovera
 Ring is inserted for 3 weeks followed by 1 week without ring in place. The ring is then reinserted for the first 21 days of subsequent 28-day cycles. One system provides contraception for 13 28-day cycles (1 year). Not yet adequately evaluated in women with BMI >29 kg/m2.
