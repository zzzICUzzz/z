Whether to obtain MRI to assess ACL
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging; ACL: anterior cruciate ligament.
* Patients who have sustained injuries due to major trauma (eg, motor vehicle collision) should be assessed in the emergency department.
Other common injuries sustained from non-contact knee trauma include tears of the lateral or medial collateral ligaments or a meniscus.
