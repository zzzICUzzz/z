Pearly penile papules
(A) Small white papules are oriented in a few rows on the corona of the penis.
(B) Whitish pink cobblestone appearance in a few rows with central dotted or comma-like vessels in each papule. The papules are further surrounded by whitish crescent-shaped rims.
(C) Prominent structures of hyperplastic papillary dermis and dilatation of vascular spaces. The epidermis shows mild acanthosis with orthokeratosis.
