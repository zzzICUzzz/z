Abnormalities of the tympanic membrane that are not cholesteatoma
White abnormalities of the tympanic membrane that are not cholesteatoma. There is a shallow retraction of the pars flaccida (asterisk).
T: tympanosclerosis; B: bone (promontory below, stapes head above); F: foreign body (a white tympanostomy tube).
