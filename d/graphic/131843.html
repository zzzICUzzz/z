Sacral fracture CT: Zone one injury
The CT images above show a Zone I sacral fracture (neural foramina not affected).The injury was sustained by a62-year-old man involved ina motorcycle accident. The coronal reformatted CT image (A) and 3D CT reconstruction (B)show a fracture of the right sacrumthat does not involvethe neural foramina (arrow).
