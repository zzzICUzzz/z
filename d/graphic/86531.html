Blastoid variant of mantle cell lymphoma
Blastoid variant of mantle cell lymphoma reveals medium-sized lymphoid cells with large irregular nuclei and a dispersed chromatin pattern. Nucleoli are seen in a few tumor cells. Hematoxylin and eosin, 60x magnification.
