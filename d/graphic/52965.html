Rectal prolapse reduction
(A) The child is placed prone in the knee-chest position on the examination table or the parent's lap.
(B) Hold the prolapsed edges with a gauze pad. Using lubricated gloves, apply gentle but firm pressure to reduce the prolapsed mucosa.
(C) Digital rectal examination is performed after reduction to ensure that reduction is complete.

If the rectal prolapse is very large and/or difficult to reduce, application of up to one-half cup (113 g) of sugar can reduce the size of the swelling and allow for manual reduction.
