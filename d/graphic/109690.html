Newborn with congenital Zika syndrome
Newborn infant congenitally infected with Zika virus. Clinical features of congenital Zika syndrome include microcephaly, facial disproportion, hypertonia/spasticity, hyperreflexia, seizures,irritability, arthrogryposis, club feet, ocular abnormalities, and hearing loss.
Reproduced with permission from: Miranda-Filho Dde B, Martelli CM, Ximenes RA, et al. Initial Description of the Presumed Congenital Zika Syndrome. Am J Public Health 2016; 106(4):598-600. Copyright 2016 American Public Health Association. All rights reserved.
