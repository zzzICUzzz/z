Histopathologic features of melasma: Fontana-Masson and Mel-5 staining
(A) Fontana-Masson stain of melasma skin showing increased epidermal melanin and dermal melanophages (original magnification 40).
(B) Fontana-Masson stain of normal adjacent skin for comparison (original magnification x40).
(C) Mel-5 immunostain of melasma skin showing enlarged, intensely stained melanocytes with prominent dendrites (original magnification 40).
(D) Mel-5 immunostain of adjacent normal skin for comparison (original magnification 40).
From: Grimes PE, Yamada N, Bhawan J. Light microscopic, immunohistochemical, and ultrastructural alterations in patients with melasma. Am J Dermatopathol 2005; 27:96.
