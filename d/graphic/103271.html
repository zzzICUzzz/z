Location of the mandibular coronoid process during temporomandibular dislocation
When the TMJ is anteriorly dislocated, the mandible is displaced forward away from its resting position. The anterior part of the ramus and coronoid process become prominent (arrow) and palpable.
TMJ: temporomandibular joint.
