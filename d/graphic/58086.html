Dissection of the anterior neck. Anterior view. The fascia has been removed, and the muscles on the left side have been reflected to show the hyoid bone, thyroid gland, and structures related to the carotid sheath (carotid artery, internal jugular vein [IJV], vagus nerve [CN X], and deep cervical lymph nodes).
