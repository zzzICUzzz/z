Psychogenic excoriation
Sharply demarcated erosions, ulcerations, and purpuric patches in a 15-year-old girl with anxiety disorder. Lesions were restricted to areas she could reach and healed completely after a week of occlusive dressing. All lesions cleared following mental health counseling.
