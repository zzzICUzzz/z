Water's view of facial bones
Zygomaticomaxillary complex fracture on the left. Water's view shows soft tissue swelling over the entire face. However, there is asymmetry between the bony structures. Notice the fractures of the floor of the orbit (open arrow), lateral maxillary wall (long arrow), and lateral wall of the orbit (arrowhead) on the left.
