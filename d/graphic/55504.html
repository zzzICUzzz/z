Heart rate and blood pressure patterns observed in head-up tilt table testing
Shown are the heart rate and blood pressure responses seen during tilt table testing in patients with various etiologies of syncope including classic neurocardiogenic syncope, pure autonomic failure, or POTS.
HR: heart rate; BP: blood pressure; POTS: postural orthostatic tachycardia syndrome.
