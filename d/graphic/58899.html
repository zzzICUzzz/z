Cases of disease except as otherwise indicated for invasive Hib disease and invasive pneumococcal disease. Dashed blue lines indicate the approximate date of routine childhood immunization against the disease.
Hib: Haemophilus influenzae type b; PCV7: 7-valent pneumococcal conjugate vaccine.
* The time period for this graph precedes the measles outbreaks that occurred across the United States during and after 2008.
Incidence per 100,000 children under five years of age.
