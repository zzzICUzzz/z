Thyroid suppression scan
123-I thyroid scan of an indeterminate thyroid nodule, after 4 weeks of T4 therapy.The nodule (outlined in white) overlies an area of relatively increased isotope concentration, indicating autonomy, since uptake is suppressed in the rest of the gland.
123-I: iodine-123; SSN: suprasternal notch; T4: levothyroxine.
