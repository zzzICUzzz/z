Hazard (risk) of stillbirth for singleton births without congenital anomalies by maternal age and gestational age, 2001-2002
The hazard of stillbirth at each gestation week was estimated by the number of stillbirths occurring during that week divided by the number of ongoing pregnancies at the beginning of that week minus half of the live births in that week.
