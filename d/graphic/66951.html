Normal fetal bowel at 24 weeks of gestationÂ (A and B) and 27 weeks of gestation (C and D).
(A) T2w coronal image shows high-signal, fluid-filled loops of bowel throughout the abdomen. Minimal low-signal meconium can be seen in the pelvis.
(B) T1w coronal image better demonstrates high-signal meconium filling the rectosigmoid and descending colon.
(C) T2w coronal image shows low-signal meconium in the rectosigmoid.
(D) T1w coronal image better demonstrates high-signal meconium filling the rectosigmoid.
