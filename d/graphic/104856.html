Hypnagogic foot tremor on polysomnography
This figure shows a 30-secondepoch of a PSGwith hypnogogic foot tremor. The PSG showsa series ofEMG bursts in the leg channels that are 250 to 1000 milliseconds long with frequencies between 0.3 Hz and 4.0 Hz (dashed box), and the EEG shows drowsiness or sleep patterns consistent with stage N1.
EOG: electrooculography; EEG: electroencephalography; electromyography; ECG: electrocardiography; PSG: polysomnography.
