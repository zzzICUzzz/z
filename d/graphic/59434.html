Schematic illustration of a parasternal short-axis echocardiographic view showing ventricular septal defects
This schematic of the parasternal short-axis echocardiographic view shows the location of the membranous septum (MS; blue) with membranous defect (M), the infundibular septum (IS; yellow) with infundibular defect (I), and atrioventricular septum (AVS; pink).
R: right coronary cusp of the aortic valve; N: noncoronary cusp; L: left coronary cusp; LA: left atrium; RA: right atrium; TV: tricuspid valve; RV: right ventricle; MPA: main pulmonary artery.
