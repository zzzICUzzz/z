Juvenile idiopathic arthritis radiograph: Joint destruction
Radiograph of a 15-year-old male with a four-year course of incompletely controlled systemic symptoms and hip arthritis. It demonstrates a pattern of bilaterally symmetric hip joint-space narrowing and erosive changes of the femoral heads and acetabulae.
