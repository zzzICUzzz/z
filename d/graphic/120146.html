Healing gastrocnemius tear ultrasound
The ultrasound images above show longitudinal and transverse views of a medial gastrocnemius tear. Organizing hematoma and tissue (arrow) consistent with thesubacute phase of healing can be seen, as can normal gastrocnemius (delta)and normalsoleus (asterisk).
