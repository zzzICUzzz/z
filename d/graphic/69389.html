Gastric component of the LES
The proximal margin of the lower esophageal sphincter (LES) extends up to and a short distance proximal to the squamocolumnar junction (not shown). The distal margin of the LES is more difficult to define, but careful anatomic studies suggest that it is composed of elements of the gastric musculature, the opposing clasp, and sling fibers of the gastric cardia.
