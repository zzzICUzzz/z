Elastic recoil of central vein stenosis treated with a stent
The angiograms show the treatment of elastic recoil of a central vein stenosis with a stent. A subclavian vein stenosis is shown (A; arrow).In spite of total effacement of the angioplasty balloon (B; arrow),marked recoil occurred (C; arrow). Following placement of a stent (D; double arrow), the vein waswide open with improved flow.
