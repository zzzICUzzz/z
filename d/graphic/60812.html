Proximal humerus fracture-dislocation
The antero-posterior radiograph above shows a fracture dislocation of the proximal humerus. The glenoid and a large fragment of the greater tuberosity are easily seen adjacent to the humeral head. Fracture dislocations require urgent orthopedic referral.
