Patient age

Less than 30 years old (0 points)

30 to 50 years old (1 point)

More than 50 years old (2 points)
Extremity injury type

Low-energy wounds such as stab, civilian gunshot, or simple fracture (1 point)

Medium-energy wounds such as dislocations; multiple or open fractures (2 points)

High-energy wounds such as crush, close-range shotgun, or military gunshot wound (3 points)

Very high-energy wounds such as above, but with gross contamination or soft tissue avulsion (4 points)
Ischemia of wounded limb

Reduced pulse but normal perfusion for less than 6 hours (1 point)

Reduced pulse but normal perfusion for more than 6 hours (2 points)

No pulse with poor capillary refill and parasthesias for less than 6 hours (2 points)

No pulse with poor capillary refill and parasthesias for more than 6 hours (4 points)

Cool, numb extremity for less than 6 hours (3 points)

Cool, numb extremity for more than 6 hours (6 points)
Hypotension and shock

Systolic BP greater than 90 mmHg (0 points)

Transient hypotension (1 point)

Persistent hypotension (2 points)
