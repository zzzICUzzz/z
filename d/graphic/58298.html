Hyperextension exercise
The patient should lie face down on the floor or on a firm bed with a pillow under the abdomen and hips. The arms should be resting at the sides. The chin should be held in or neutral. The patient should inhale and raise the head, neck, chest (as a block), and arms a few inches from the floor. Hold for five seconds. RepeatÂ 5 to 10 times.
