Stage I posterior vaginal wall prolapse
When the levator ani no longer holds the vagina closed, the anterior wall pressure no longer balances the posterior wall pressure. Stress is placed on the connective tissue support structures, as the pressure on the rectum is not counterbalanced. A rectocele may subsequently develop (arrow).
