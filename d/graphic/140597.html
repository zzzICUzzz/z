Electroencephalography in an infant with infantile epileptic spasms syndrome (IESS)
(A) and (B) show 7-month-old boy with infantile epileptic spasms syndrome. The interictal EEG (A) shows a hypsarhythmia pattern. The ictal recording (B) shows a high-amplitude sharp wave followed by a relative decrement with a muscle contraction on EMG.
EEG: electroencephalography; EMG: electromyography.
From: Zuberi SM, Wirrell E, Yozawitz E, et al. ILAE classification and definition of epilepsy syndromes with onset in neonates and infants: Position statement by the ILAE Task Force on Nosology and Definitions. Epilepsia 2022; 63:1349.
