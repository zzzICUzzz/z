Expression of saliva from submandibular glands
Video of the floor of mouth demonstrating the effect of massaging the submandibular glands (pressing up medially under the midportion of the mandible) to show delivery of saliva from both submandibular ducts before botulinum toxin was infused into the left gland; and again 18 days after the botulinum toxin had been infused.
