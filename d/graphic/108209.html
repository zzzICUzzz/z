Davos technique to reduce anterior glenohumeral dislocation
To perform the Davos technique, the patient relaxes their shoulder and arm muscles and then extends their head back, letting the shoulders roll forward with the arms extended.The techniqueis performed with the wrists bound together around the ipsilateral knee, and the foot braced, as shown in the photograph.
