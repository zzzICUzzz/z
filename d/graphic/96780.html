41-year-old woman with spinal schistosomiasis
(A) Sagittal T2-weighted magnetic resonance image (MRI) shows diffuse signal prolongation and expansion of distal spinal cord and conus (arrow).
(B) Sagittal contrast-enhanced T1-weighted MRI shows central linear enhancement surrounded by multiple punctate enhancing nodules (arrows) that form masslike structure.
