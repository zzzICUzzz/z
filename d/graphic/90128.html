Clinical features of a melanocytic nevus located in the interdigital space of the foot
Melanocytic nevus located in the fourth interdigital space of the right foot of a 23-year-old patient. This brownish-black macule, 5 mm in diameter, shows virtually no irregularity in shape or color.
