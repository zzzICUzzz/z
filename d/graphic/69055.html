Child with less severe presentation of acute epiglottitis
Although not widely appreciated, epiglottitis caused by Haemophilus influenzae type b varies widely along the spectrum of severity. This one-year-old infant appears mildly anxious but looks much less toxic than patients with the "classic" presentation.
