MRI findings in a patient with herpes simplex encephalitis
(Upper panels) Magnetic resonance (MR) images of the brain of a patient with herpes simplex virus encephalitis at two day after admission. Abnormal signals were seen in the right temporal lobe and insular cortex.
(Lower panels) MR images showing that abnormal signals had spread to the left side at 15 days after admission.
