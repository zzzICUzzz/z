Differential muscle sensitivity to rocuronium
Approximate time course of twitch height reduction and recovery at different muscles after administration of rocuronium 0.6 mg/kg IV.
Larynx: laryngeal adductors (vocal cords); CS: corrugator supercilii muscle (eyebrow); Abd: abdominal muscles; OO: orbicularis oculi muscle (eyelid); GH: geniohyoid muscle (upper airway); AP: adductor pollicis muscle (thumb); IV: intravenous.
