Scapular anomalies in skeletal dysplasia
Schematic presentation of the characteristic radiographic appearance of the scapula in the different entities. The drawings are from the reported cases and therefore represent different ages.
(A) Luton type of PLSD.
(B) San Diego type of PLSD.
(C) Torrance type of PLSD.
(D) Campomelic dysplasia.
(E) Kyphomelic dysplasia.
(F) Antley-Bixler syndrome.
(G) SRP type II (Majewski).
(H) SRP type III (Verma-Naumoff).
PLSD: platyspondylic lethal skeletal dysplasia; SRP: short-rib polydactyly syndrome.
