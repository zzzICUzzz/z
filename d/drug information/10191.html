Use: Labeled Indications

Pantothenic acid deficiency: Dietary supplement to treat pantothenic acid deficiency.

Metabolism/Transport Effects

None known.

Drug Interactions

There are no known significant interactions.

Pregnancy Considerations

Water soluble vitamins cross the placenta (IOM, 1998).

Breastfeeding Considerations

Pantothenic acid is found in breast milk; concentrations may be increased by use of maternal supplements (IOM, 1998)

Dietary Considerations

Dietary adequate intake (AI) (IOM 1998):

1 to 6 months: 1.7 mg/day

7 to 12 months: 1.8 mg/day

1 to 3 years: 2 mg/day

Mechanism of Action

Pantothenic acid is required for the synthesis and maintenance of coenzyme A.

Pharmacokinetics (Adult Data Unless Noted)

Absorption: Absorbed in the intestine

Metabolism: Hydrolyzed in the intestine to coenzyme A

Excretion: Urine
