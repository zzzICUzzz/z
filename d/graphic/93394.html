Photograph of a pelvic pressure pack, as constructed from an x-ray cassette drape, sterile gauze rolls, and an intravenous infusion setup.
(A) "Dome" of the pack.
(B, C, D) "Neck" of the pack.
(E) Gauze rolls.
(F) Intravenous tubing.
(G) Intravenous fluid bag (1 L).
