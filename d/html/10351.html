  • Mucolytic Agent
Reported Use

Adjunctive mucolytic therapy in respiratory tract disorders characterized by excessive or viscous mucous secretions

Dosage Range

Oral:

Children 2 to 5 years: 100 mg twice daily or 62.5 to 125 mg 4 times daily.

Children 6 to 11 years: 100 to 250 mg 3 times daily.

Children 12 to <15 years: 100 to 750 mg 3 times daily.

Adolescents ≥15 years and Adults: 750 mg to 2.25 g/day in 3 to 4 divided doses.

Product Availability

Product available in various countries; not currently available in the US

Dosage Forms

Capsule, Oral: 375 mg, 500 mg

Packet, Oral: 750 mg/10 mL

Solution, Oral: 50 mg/mL

Syrup, Oral: 100 mg/5 mL, 250 mg/5 mL
