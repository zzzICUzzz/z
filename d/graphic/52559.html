Percutaneous transtracheal ventilation catheter adapters
A 3-mL syringe with the plunger removed may be used as an adapter between the catheter and a 7.5 mm ID endotracheal tube. Alternately, a 3.0 mm ID endotracheal tube connector can be inserted directly into the catheter hub. Both methods can be used to connect the catheter to a standard self-inflating resuscitation bag.
