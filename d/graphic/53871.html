HACE MRI
With HACE, MRI reveals characteristic intense T2 signal in the white matter, especially the splenium of the corpus callosum, with no grey matter lesions. The MRI may remain abnormal for days to weeks.
HACE: high altitude cerebral edema; MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
