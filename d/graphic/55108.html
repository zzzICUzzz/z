Granulomatous lung involvement in CGD
High resolution computed tomography (HRCT) scan in a patient with chronic granulomatous disease, nonproductive cough, and pleuritic chest pain. Nodular area of consolidation in right middle lobe was persistent for several months despite antibiotic therapy. Lung biopsy revealed granulomatous inflammation. Bilateral areas of decreased vascularity and attenuation are also noted.
CGD: chronic granulomatous disease.
