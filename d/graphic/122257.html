Salivary gland atrophy with chronic obstruction
A large distal left submandibular duct stone in a patient with a 15-year history of pain and swelling that had resolved 5 years previously, presumably due to gland atrophy (as seen on CT).
SMG: submandibular gland; CT: computed tomography.
