Open forearm fracture
Open fracture. This 8-year-old boy fell on his hand and was referred to the emergency department with a deformed forearm (Panel A) due to a distal radial/ulnar fracture. After removal from his splint a small ecchymosis and laceration were noted over the ventral aspect of his forearm (Panel B).
