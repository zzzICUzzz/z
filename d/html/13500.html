Notes
  • This risk assessment tool is based on the Cox regression model of proportional hazards.
  • It may be applied to men between the ages of 55 and 84 who have had no prior history of stroke.
  • Cardiovascular disease includes coronary disease, heart failure, or claudication.
  • BP : blood pressure; LV : left ventricular.


Equations used

Risk_factor_diff = (Age * 0.0505) + (Systolic_BP * 0.014) + HTN_Med + DM + Cig + CVD + AFib + LVH - 5.677
Risk_during_period = 100 * (1 - Risk_period_factor e (Risk_factor_diff) )
