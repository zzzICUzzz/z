Pressure tracings used to calculate FFr. Aortic pressure (red) and sensor-wire pressure (green) at rest (left) and after adensoine IV (at yellow arrow) show maximal separation at hyperemia where FFR is calculated as Pd/Pa = 65/90 = 0.72.
