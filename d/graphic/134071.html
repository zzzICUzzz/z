Major papilla with diverticulum
The endoscopic pictures demonstrate the position of the major papilla in relationship to a diverticulum in the second portion of the duodenum. The papilla may be located inside the diverticulum (panel A, pictured during guidewire insertion), at the margin of the diverticulum (panel B), or outside the diverticulum (panel C).
