Normal lateral radiograph of the elbow of a 2-year-old child
The anterior fat pad is readily seen (arrow); the posterior fat pad is not visible. A line drawn along the anterior cortex of the humerus intersects the capitellum in its middle third (solid line). A line drawn along the axis of the radius also passes through the center of the capitellum (dashed line).
