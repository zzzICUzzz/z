Patch myringoplasty
Otoscopic images showing the sequence of patch myringoplasty following removal of a TT.
(A) The TT in place in the TM.
(B and C) The TT is removed with a forceps.
(D) The residual perforation in the TM.
(E and F)A patch is placed over the perforation.
TT: tympanostomy tube; TM: tympanic membrane.
