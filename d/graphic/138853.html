TB: tuberculosis; WHO: World Health Organization; CDC: United States Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.

* The shortened (4-month regimen) may be used for treatment for nonsevere, smear-negative, presumed drug-susceptible disease (defined as pulmonary TB confined to one lobe). Exclusions include cavitary disease, miliary TB, complex pleural effusion, clinically significant airway obstruction, and peripheral lymph node involvement.
