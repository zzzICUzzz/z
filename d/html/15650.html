BISAP score interpretation


0 to 2 points: Lower mortality (<2%)
3 to 5 points: Higher mortality (>15%)


Notes
  • SIRS is present when two or more of the following are found: 1) Temperature <36°C [96.8°F] or >38°C [100.4°F]; 2) respirations >20/min or PaCO 2 <32 mmHg; 3) heart rate >90/min; 4) WBC <4000/mm 3 or WBC >12,000/mm 3 or more than 10% bands found on blood smear.
  • Please see appropriate UpToDate topic reviews for further information.
  • BUN : blood urea nitrogen; SIRS : systemic inflammatory response syndrome; PaCO2 : partial pressure of carbon dioxide; WBC : white blood cell count.
