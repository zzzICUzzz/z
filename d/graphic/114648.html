Deltoid injection site
The arm should be completely exposed and lifted slightly out to the side (which causes the subdeltoid bursa to slide under the acromion). Identify the shoulder tip (acromion) and the deltoid tuberosity (ie, the site of insertion of the deltoid). Draw an imaginary inverted triangle between the acromion and the deltoid tuberosity. The correct injection site is in the middle one-third of this triangle (ie, in the center of the deltoid muscle, midway between the acromion and the deltoid tuberosity).
