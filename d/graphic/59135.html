Pseudoxanthoma elasticum
Symmetric, confluent, yellow papules and redundant skin folds are present in this 38-year-old woman. The characteristic plucked chicken skin changes on the neck of patients with pseudoxanthoma elasticum are seen. An eye examination showed the characteristic angioid streaks.
