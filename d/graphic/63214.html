Spigelian hernia
Herniation of fat through a defect in the aponeurosis between the left rectus abdominis (arrow) and the aponeurosis of the left transversus abdominis and internal oblique muscles. The lateral margin of the hernia sac is the external oblique muscle and fascia (arrowhead).
