Collagenous colitis high power histology
High power view ofa randomcolon biopsy specimen in a patient with collagenous colitisreveals lymphocytic infiltration of the superficial epithelium and an excess of chronic inflammatory cells in the lamina propria. The pink stripe noticed on low power viewis subepithelial collagen deposition (arrow), which has entrapped superficial blood vessels (circle).
