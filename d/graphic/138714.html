Articular process view for lumbar neuraxial ultrasound
In the articular process view, a scalloped hyperechoic line is visualized, corresponding to the overlap of superior and inferior articular processes. This pattern is referred to as the "camel hump," sign. For further detail refer to UpToDate content on neuraxial ultrasound for anesthesia.
