Cytological features of CAR-T cells
The morphology of circulating CAR-T cells may demonstrate activated/atypical features that should not be confused with circulating malignant cells. The first three panels illustrate circulating CAR-T cells (Wright-Giemsa stain), while the panel in the lower right illustrates flow cytometry with CD3 (vertical axis) andanti-idiotype antibody specific for CTL019 (horizontal axis).
From: Brooks S, Frey N, Porter D, et al. The cytological features of CAR(T) cells. Br J Haematol 2016; 175(3):366. https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1111/bjh.14286.
