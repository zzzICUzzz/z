Radiologic response to therapy in a patient with pulmonary paracoccidioidomycosis
Complete radiologic response in a patient with chronic paracoccidioidomycosis who was treated with voriconazole. A progressive improvement of the pulmonary radiologic images is clearly observed during treatment, and a follow-up stabilization pattern is achieved.
EOT: end of treatment.
