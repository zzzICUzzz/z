Donut mastopexy
Donut mastopexy lumpectomy, medial breast lesion.
(A) Preoperative marking including marking of region to be removed based on preoperative bracketing wires and concentric circles for skin donut excision.
(B) Initial skin incision.
(C) Elevation of skin envelope dissecting in mastectomy plane.
(D) Mobilization of full-thickness wedge of disease-bearing broglandular tissue.
(E) Excised specimen and resulting broglandular defect.
(F) Mastopexy closure.
(G) Skin closure.
(H) Postoperative result.
