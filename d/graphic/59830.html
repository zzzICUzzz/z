Schematic of echo findings in EMF
Schematic of the two-dimensional, four-chamber apical view findings in 10 patients with endmyocardial disease. Numbers on the right and left indicate the number of patients with each finding.
IVC: inferior vena cava; MOT: preserved or increased inward motion of the obliterative process; OBL: obliteration; RFL: increased reflectance of the obliterative surface; THICK: increased thickening of the basal inflow tract papillary muscle and/or posterior atrioventricular valve.
