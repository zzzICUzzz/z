Copenhagen hip adduction exercises
The Copenhagenhip adductor exercisesshown above reduce the risk of adductor strain.Asthe adductors become stronger, athletes progress from the basic exercise (3A-B) to intermediate(2A-B) andmore advanced(1A-B) versions. For each exercise shown,A is the starting/ending position and B is the mid-position.
