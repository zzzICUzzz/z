Leukemic lines (metaphyseal "bands" or lucency)
Anteroposterior radiograph of right knee in a 6-year-old patient presented with limp and hypercalcemia shows metaphyseal lucent lines (arrows), prompting bone marrow aspiration and yielding a diagnosis of pre B-cell acute lymphoblastic leukemia.
