Notes
  • The formula for predicted body weight used in the calculator is the same as that used in the UpToDate calculator for ideal body weight. [1,2]
  • The UpToDate ideal body weight calculator also provides adjusted body weight, which may be helpful in drug dosing calculations for patients with obesity.
  • For more information on the use of the predicted body weight calculation, please refer to UpToDate topic reviews on mechanical ventilation strategies for acute respiratory distress syndrome.
  • To convert from cm to in, divide by 2.54; to convert from in to cm, multiply by 2.54.


Equations used

Predicted body weight [kg] = Base weight for sex (biologic) [kg] + (2.3 * (Height [in] - 60))
