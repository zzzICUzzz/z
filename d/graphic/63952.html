Transverse view of aortic rupture on TEE
This TEE transverse view demonstrates a traumatic subadventitial rupture of the aortic isthmus. There is a thick and highly mobile medial flap (arrow) associated with localized deformity of the anterolateral aortic wall (arrowheads) from acute formation of a false aneurysm.
