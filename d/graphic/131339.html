Spontaneous termination of sinoatrial node reentrant tachycardia (SANRT)
12-lead electrocardiogram at sweep speed 100 mm/sec showing spontaneous termination of SANRT (200 bpm) into sinus tachycardia (155 bpm). P waves during pathologic tachycardia (thin arrows) are identical to those during sinus rhythm (thick arrows). P wavesare partially superimposed on the prior T wave. The PR interval during the tachyarrhythmia (93 msec) is inappropriately long and is identical to the PR interval in sinus rhythm.
SANRT: sinoatrial node reentrant tachycardia; bpm: beats per minute.
