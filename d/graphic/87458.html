Meckel's diverticulum age distribution
The cases of Meckel's diverticulum in children include the more common presentations, such as bleeding or diverticulitis, as well as a variety of other presentations, such as persistent omphalomesenteric ducts that result in chronic umbilical drainage, intussusception, and volvulus around a Meckel's band.
