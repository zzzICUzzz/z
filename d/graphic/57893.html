Extensive hidradenitis suppurativa with multiple skin tunnels
This picture shows the initial surgical unroofing of the inflamed hidradenitis suppurativa nodule, the lateral extension of the skin tunnel, and an additional inferior lateral skin tunnel. Gentle probing with blunt scissors has identified another skin tunnel that requires unroofing with beveled skin edges.
