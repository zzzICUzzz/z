Imaging of paracoccidioidomycosis
Images of paracoccidioidomycosis. A. Chest radiograph demonstrating a "butterfly wing" pattern. B. Chest radiograph with a diffuse nodular and micronodular pattern. C. Chest computed tomography (CT) scan demonstrating multiple cavities. D. Abdominal CT scan demonstrating enlarged adrenal glands bilaterally. E and F. Head CT demonstrating hypodense ring-enhancing lesions.
